Ini Oguntola


Ini Oguntola is an incoming Ph.D. student in Machine Learning at Carnegie Mellon University. He was previously a student at MIT, graduating in 2019 with a double B.S. in Computer Science and Mathematics as well as an M.Eng. degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science. During his time at MIT he also worked at Google (summers 2016 and 2017) and Microsoft Research (summer 2018).

Ini is also a composer and multi-instrumentalist, playing the saxophone, clarinet, and piano. At MIT he was an Emerson Fellow, studying performance and composition under the tutelage of Billy Novick and John Harbison. While at MIT he has performed with a variety of musical artists, including Jacob Collier, Miguel Zenon, Don Byron, and Sean Jones.
